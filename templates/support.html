Support

Raise a Ticket

You will need to create or log in to an EGI Check-in account to submit a ticket as we use the GGUS helpdesk service for managing tickets. You will be asked to do so before starting your ticket.

Filling out your ticket

Please follow the instructions below to ensure your ticket is filled out correctly.

Assign the right support unit

  1. Make sure to assign the ticket to the "PITHIA e-Science Centre" support unit as we will not receive a notification of your ticket otherwise, and, due to how GGUS Helpdesk works, may also not be able to classify your ticket in the future.

    The support unit can be assigned from the provided dropdown menu for the Assign to support unit field in the bottom-right of the ticket form, within the "PITHIA Community" dropdown group.

Attach files

  1. If applicable, attach relevant files (e.g., a file that failed metadata validation) or screenshots of any errors to help us to better understand your problem.
