The e-Science Centre uses the GGUS helpdesk service as a way to provide technical support for the e-Science Centre. An EGI Check-in account is required to use GGUS.

How to Submit a Ticket

  1. To submit a ticket, go to the Submit ticket page in GGUS. You will be asked to sign in with EGI Check-in if you haven't done so already.
  2. Fill out all of the required fields (these will have a * next to the field names) for the ticket.
  3. Select the PITHIA e-Science Centre Support unit (under PITHIA Community) for the Assign to support unit field; we will not receive notification of your ticket otherwise.
  4. Submit the form.
