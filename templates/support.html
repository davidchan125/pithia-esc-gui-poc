Raise a Ticket

You can raise a ticket via the external GGUS helpdesk service. To use GGUS, you will need to sign up/log in with an EGI Check-in account, which GGUS will ask for before starting your ticket.

Assign your Ticket to the PITHIA e-Science Centre Support Unit

  • Assign your ticket to the PITHIA e-Science Centre support unit (SU) to ensure it reaches us.

    If appropriate, also consider attaching screenshots and/or files to help us better understand the ticket.

    GGUS Ticket Form Guide

    From the ticket form:

    1. Open the Assign to support unit dropdown menu in the bottom-right of the ticket form.
    2. Select "PITHIA e-Science Centre" (under the "PITHIA Community" group) within the menu.
