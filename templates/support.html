Support

The e-Science Centre uses the GGUS helpdesk service for submitting and managing technical support queries. You will need to create or log in to your EGI Check-in account before starting your ticket.

If this is your first time submitting a ticket, please ensure that you have read what to do when filling out your ticket.

Filling out your ticket

  1. Make sure to assign the ticket to the "PITHIA e-Science Centre" support unit as we will not receive a notification of your ticket otherwise, and, due to how GGUS Helpdesk works, may also not be able to classify your ticket in the future.

    The support unit can be assigned from the provided dropdown menu for the Assign to support unit field in the bottom-right of the ticket form, within the "PITHIA Community" dropdown group.

Suggestions

  1. Attach relevant files (e.g., a file that failed metadata validation) or screenshots of your problem to help us to better understand its nature and help with resolving it.
